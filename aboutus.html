MedCycl is a 501 (c) 3 not-for-profit organization that collects medical supplies and equipment from healthcare facilities and manufacturers in the Southern California area. We sort and distribute these supplies to provide targeted support to disenfranchised community health centers globally. Our goal is to utilize these unused and discarded supplies to provide needed resources to these communities. By sending supplies, MedCycl aims to support missions and advocate for global health issues.

Our Values

Discovery

Implementing new methods of reducing waste within the healthcare system, MedCycl fosters a sense of discovery to drive our group into finding innovative approaches to answering this problem.
